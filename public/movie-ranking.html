The Best Movies According to Ankita

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dangal

This movie encourages us all to keep fighting against the odds and pursue our dreams with unwavering determination.

3 Idiots

This movie teaches us that a certificate is nothing more than a piece of paper.

Goopy Gyne Bagha Byne

The film subtly criticizes social hierarchies, power dynamics, and the destructive nature of war through the characters and situations.
